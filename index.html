Hi, I'm Nabeel Chollampat. I'm a writer and educator based in Syracuse, NY.

I currently teach freshman-level writing at SUNY ESF, as well as serving as coordinator of the student writing center. I graduated from Syracuse with an MFA in Fiction in 2025.

Before Syracuse, I lived in Brooklyn, where I worked for Gimlet Media for 4 years.

I graduated from the University of Michigan in 2018 with degrees in Math and English. I also served as editor of the arts section of the student newspaper, The Michigan Daily.

I write, teach, and am a freelance audio editor and producer.
