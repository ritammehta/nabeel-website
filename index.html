About

Hi! I'm Nabeel Chollampat. I'm a teacher, content writer, and podcast producer based in NYC.

I write copy and content marketing materials for startups and thought leaders, and produce podcasts for clients as varied as energy task forces and org design consultancies. I also write a weekly comedy newsletter with a friend.

Previously, I worked at Spotify and Gimlet Media in finance, operations, and internal comms.

I was most recently the Director of the Writing Resource Center at SUNY ESF, and a Visiting Lecturer at SUNY ESF and Syracuse University, where I received my MFA in Creative Writing - Fiction.
